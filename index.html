As a Frontend Developer, I am driven by the problems I get to solve everyday, and the number of ideas that I am able to explore. I love how various components - layout, typography, colours, imagery, language and data - are merged to bring concepts to life.

Over the past 3 years, I have built web applications using modern frontend frameworks across a range of sectors including finance, retail, charity, transport, and hospitality, to name a few. Through these experiences, I have developed a solid understanding of the core principles of user-centred design for the web and product development - from concept to deployment. Working in the financial space further deepened my knowledge of this as I was constantly building for security and trust, and also learnt how to prioritise ease of use and efficiency.
