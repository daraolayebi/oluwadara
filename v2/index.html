my name is dara and i'm a frontend developer and user interface designer

i have spent the last 3+ years building digital products in numerous sectors - finance, retail, events, charity and agriculture, to name a few. my experiences have given me a solid understanding of how users interact with technology, and strengthened my technical skills in JavaScript and its frontend frameworks.

when i'm not in front of my computer screen, i'm singing along to a spotify playlist, reading a good fiction novel, or exploring ideas for my next big project - check out the before i die wall.

lunchtime

after experiencing terribly long queues at the office canteen, a friend and i did some brainstorming and a few weeks later, 'lunchtime' was born.

This web app made it quick and easy for staff to choose a meal, make payment, and get their order delivered straight to their desks. in the period when it was in use, there were over 2000 orders placed by staff.

the lagos leather fair is an annual event created to showcase West Africa's leading leather designers and to build awareness around Nigeria's fast-growing leather industry. the event website presents visitors with the entire schedule of events.
